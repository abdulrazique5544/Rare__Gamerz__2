Our Games

Free Fire

Free Fire is a fast-paced battle royale game where players compete to be the last one standing on a remote island. It offers intense action, strategic gameplay, and exciting character customization.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to explore, build, and create in a blocky, pixelated world. With survival and creative modes, it offers endless possibilities for adventure and creativity.

PUBG

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is a battle royale game known for its realistic graphics and immersive gameplay. Players scavenge for weapons and gear while competing to be the last survivor on a vast map.
